<p><strong>AND IN THEM ALL THEIR POSTERITY;</strong> for even such as were not then born are judged and condemned in them, Gen. iii. 16, &amp;c. so that without doubt they also sinned in them, and at the same time with them. Rom. v. 12. <i>by one man sin entered into the world,</i> v. 15. <i>through the offence of one many be dead;</i> and v. 16. <i>the judgement was by one to condemnation;</i> v. 17. <i>by one man's offence death reigned by one;</i> and v. 18. <i>by the offence of one man judgement came upon all men to condemnation;</i> and v. 19. <i>by one man's disobedience many were made sinners,</i> 1 Cor. xv. 22. <i>in Adam all die;</i> undoubtedly therefore all sinned in Adam.
